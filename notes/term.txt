[OFF : JIBE : Admin : SURVEY] After saving Add/Edit Certificate with more than 10 digits in Term field ,the value in Term is not displayed.




Steps:

1. Technical>Admin>Survey and Certificate Settings>Survey Certificate.

2. Click on Add/Edit Survey Certificate.

3. Fill all the details and save.

4. Observe the Term Column in Survey Certificate page.

ISSUE: After saving Add/Edit Certificate with more than 10 digits in Term field, the value in Term is not displayed.
